A Little About Me

I was born on the island of Aruba in the year 1986. It was a delightful Sunday afternoon. I weighed somewhere around 8 lbs.

I spent the first eight years of my life living in Aruba. I grew up speaking Dutch and learned English by watching TV. Some of the shows I watched are:

  • Saved by the Bell
  • Mickey Mouse Club
  • Dough
  • Rugrats

When I was eight my family moved to Bolivia. The first few months in Bolivia were really difficult for me. Once school started I had a hard time communicating with my classmates. I spoke no Spanish at the time, and my English left much to be desired. I was constantly accidentally using Dutch words. Eventually, I made friends and everything was great!
